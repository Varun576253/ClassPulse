==================================================
PRIMARY OBJECTIVE
=================

The objective is NOT to build the most feature-rich product.

The objective is to build the strongest possible solution for the challenge:

LEARNING GAPS AND TIMELY FEEDBACK

Challenge:

Teachers often lack practical ways to diagnose student-level learning gaps and provide timely, actionable feedback in large and mixed-ability classrooms.

Students who need support are often not identified early enough.

The final product should directly solve that problem.

==================================================
OFFICIAL CHALLENGE REQUIREMENTS
===============================

Who is affected:

* Teachers
* Students in large classrooms

Key considerations:

* Low teacher burden
* Timely feedback

Feasibility, Scalability & Pilot Readiness

Every implementation decision should improve performance against these requirements.

==========================

* Product feels like a QR generator.

Desired outcome:

* Product feels like a teacher learning-gap intervention assistant.

Challenge requirements take priority over implementation preferences.
